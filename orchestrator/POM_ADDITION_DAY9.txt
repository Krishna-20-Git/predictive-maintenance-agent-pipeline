Add this inside the <dependencies> block of orchestrator/pom.xml,
anywhere among the other <dependency> entries:

        <dependency>
            <groupId>redis.clients</groupId>
            <artifactId>jedis</artifactId>
            <version>7.1.0</version>
        </dependency>

After adding it, in IntelliJ: right-click pom.xml → Maven → Reload Project
(or click the little "m" refresh icon that appears in the top-right corner
of the editor when pom.xml has unsaved/unsynced changes).
